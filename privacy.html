Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

CattleCounter does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data, images, or location information. All processing happens entirely on your device.

Who we are

CattleCounter is developed and operated by David Tschetter. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at cattlecounter-support@pm.me.

Information we do not collect

CattleCounter does not collect or transmit:

  • Drone images or photographs of any kind
  • Location or GPS data
  • Annotation or count data
  • Device identifiers or usage analytics
  • Names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers

On-device processing

All image analysis and cattle detection runs locally on your iPad using Apple's Neural Engine. No images are uploaded to any server at any time. Your herd data stays on your device.

In-app purchases

CattleCounter offers in-app purchases processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not handle or store any payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs all purchase transactions. You can review Apple's privacy policy at apple.com/legal/privacy.

RevenueCat

CattleCounter uses RevenueCat to manage in-app purchase entitlements. RevenueCat may collect limited purchase-related data such as your App Store anonymous user identifier to verify subscription status. RevenueCat does not receive your images, location, or any other app data. You can review RevenueCat's privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Data storage

All app data — including annotated sessions, exported datasets, and settings — is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account if iCloud sync is enabled. We have no access to this data.

Children's privacy

CattleCounter is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at cattlecounter-support@pm.me
